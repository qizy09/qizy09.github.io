Open lecture platform.

Lecture Square allows lecturer easily extend their lecture and makes the synchronization between lecturer and audience easier.

Amazing social network.

Embedding Online Social Network with Real World Lectures events. Never miss a chance to know your classmates and people with similar interests.

Targets

This app mainly targets at improving user experience of lectures..
    Lecturer - Controls the presentation with their smartphone. Audience - Follow along with the slides. Associate particular slides or bullet points with custom notes, or media, such as pictures, videos, or audio.
